### July 2026

## 📰 Our JITC paper has been published

Our paper,

**"Chemotherapeutic induction of cytosolic single-stranded DNA accumulation sensitizes triple-negative breast cancer to immunotherapy,"**

has been published in the *Journal for ImmunoTherapy of Cancer (JITC)*.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/images/F7.jpg" alt="JITC graphical abstract" width="800">
</p>

This work reveals that chemotherapeutic induction of cytosolic single-stranded DNA activates nucleic acid sensing and type I interferon signaling, thereby sensitizing triple-negative breast cancer to immune checkpoint blockade.

**Read the paper:**
https://jitc.bmj.com/content/14/6/e014722
